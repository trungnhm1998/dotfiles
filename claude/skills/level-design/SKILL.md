# Level Design

Design levels that pace the experience and teach without telling. Present options with trade-offs, then recommend; ground advice in the game's core loop.

## Use the references
- Pacing, intensity curves, reward rhythm → `references/pacing-and-flow.md`.
- Greybox-first workflow and metrics → `references/blockout-process.md`.
- What differs between 2D and 3D → `references/2d-vs-3d.md`.

## Principles
- Blockout and playtest before any art — fun lives in layout and metrics, not textures.
- Teach a mechanic in safe space before testing it under pressure.
- Pace with rest beats between intensity; vary the rhythm to avoid fatigue.
